About the School

Located in Mumbai and affiliated with Mahad al-Zahra, Rawdat al-Qur’an al-Kareem blends traditional Qur’anic teachings with modern education. Our mission is to develop spiritually grounded and intellectually enriched individuals.

Curriculum Highlights

  • Qur’an Memorization (Hifz Program) with Tajweed
  • Integration of Edexcel & Cambridge curricula
  • Thematic Learning—Art, Storytelling, Robotics
  • Educational Excursions and Workshops

Student Life & Activities

We celebrate achievements through Annual Days, storytelling sessions, Mathlathons, and interactive workshops. Students flourish spiritually, academically, and socially in our nurturing environment.
